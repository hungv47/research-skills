# Industry Benchmarks (2025-2026)

Conversion rate and cost benchmarks by industry and business model. Use as starting points for target setting.

**Tier Definitions:**
- **Poor:** Bottom quartile, needs immediate attention
- **Average:** Median performance, room for improvement
- **Good:** Top 25%, solid performance
- **Excellent:** Top 10%, best-in-class

### Key Insight: The MQL→SQL Bottleneck

The MQL→SQL transition is the biggest leak in most B2B funnels:
- Average: 15-21% conversion
- Improving by 5 points can lift revenue by up to 18%
- Companies with <1 hour lead follow-up see 53% conversion vs 17% for 24+ hour follow-up

## Using Benchmarks

### When to Use Industry Benchmarks

- **Starting point**: No historical data
- **Reality check**: Validate your numbers aren't outliers
- **Goal setting**: Set targets relative to "good"
- **Investor conversations**: Show awareness of norms

### When NOT to Rely on Benchmarks

- **You have 6+ months of data**: Use your actual baselines
- **Unique business model**: Benchmarks may not apply
- **Different market**: Geographic/demographic differences
- **Outlier product**: Some products legitimately break norms

### Benchmark Sources

Most data compiled from:
- First Page Sage
- Databox
- HubSpot Research
- Benchmarkit
- Industry reports (Gartner, Forrester)
- Platform data (Google, Meta, LinkedIn)

Benchmarks update frequently. Verify with current sources for critical decisions.

### Market Cycle Adjustments

**Bull vs Bear Benchmarks:**

| Metric | Bear Market | Bull Market |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| New user growth | -5% to +10% MoM | +20% to +100% MoM |
| TVL growth | Flat to -20% | +30% to +200% |
| Transaction volume | -30% to flat | +50% to +500% |
| Retention | Lower baseline | Higher baseline |

**Strategy by market:**
- **Bear market:** Focus on retention over acquisition
- **Neutral:** Balance growth and sustainability
- **Bull market:** Capitalize on acquisition, prepare for retention

### Warning Signs

Red flags regardless of benchmarks:

| Metric | Warning Level |
|--------|---------------|
| D7 retention | <5% |
| TVL per user declining | >20% drop in 30 days |
| Transaction volume | >50% drop (non-market) |
| Community sentiment | <50% positive |
| Protocol revenue | Cannot cover costs |
